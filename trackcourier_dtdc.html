About DTDC

DTDC is one of the leading courier company in India. DTDC has the widest network coverage and the largest courier fleet in India.

Based in Bangalore, DTDC has 300 plus of its own offices and over 5,800 franchisees across the country. It was the first courier company to introduce the franchisee model in India.

You can find exact location of DTDC offices from the official website: http://www.dtdc.in/our-presence.asp
